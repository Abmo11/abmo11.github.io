Overview

Purpose

The purpose of Para Bellum Strength and Health is to provide practical health and fitness advice and information and to promote a sustainably healthy lifestyle. Information and advice will be given from my own experience and will encompass three main areas: nutrition, rest, and fitness.

Audience

This site is for the everyday person. The person who may be on a budget or have a busy schedule. The everyday mom or dad. The person who is trying to better themselves but does not know where to start. Everyone who has ever struggled with weight loss or given up on it.

Typography

Heading Font: Josefin Sans

I am taking this font from the logo. I want there to be a uniform feel to the site; this only makes sense as it will allude to the logo. I also want a clean, modern look and this will do it without pushing too far.

Paragraph Font: Quicksand

This font pairs very well with the heading font. It accommodates to all visitors as it is easy to read, it is clean, and goes with the modern look I want.
