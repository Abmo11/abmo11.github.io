Overview

Purpose

To promote and provide information about the specialized services that Temple Inn and Suites offers to meet the needs of temple patrons, and their families, who come to serve in the temple or who participate in events such as sealings, weddings, receptions, and youth trips.

Audience

The accommodations and services outlined in this site are for LDS temple patrons, workers, their family members, and anyone willing to abide by the terms and policies outlined by the company.

Typography

Heading Font: Josefin Sans

I am taking this font from the logo. I want there to be a uniform feel to the site; this only makes sense as it will allude to the logo. I also want a clean, modern look and this will do it without pushing too far.

Paragraph Font: Open Sans

This font pairs very well with the heading font. It accommodates to all visitors as it is easy to read, it is clean, and goes with the modern look I want.

Normal paragraph example

We offer a complete package for whatever your needs are in relation to temple service and accomodations. Our shuttle runs to meet the endowment schedule and our babysitting servcies provide an afforable and safe alternative for your family.

Colored paragraph example

Plan on using our free changing room for you and your fellow patrons as you transition from traveling to the temple to actually entering the temple. We want you to feel comfortable changing and freshing up in order to focus on meaningful service. Let us take care of you.
