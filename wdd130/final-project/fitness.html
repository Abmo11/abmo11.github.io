Train Smart

In order for you to gain muscle you’ll have to do some type of resistance training. This can be done with or without weights. When it comes to losing weight there is nothing that works better than endurance training. I recommend learning boxing. It is one of the most complete, full-body workouts you can do. You will gain strength, stamina, hand-eye coordination, and agility, all while losing those extra pounds. Below you will find simple to advanced workouts that have been tried and tested.

Beginner

This workout routine can be done at home. One ‘round’ of this includes all 6 exercises (as described below) and will hit every muscle in your body. This is a great balance between resistance and endurance training. No weights are required. You will want to stretch before and after. Try 3 rounds to begin.

Advanced

This workout is for the gym rat. The key here is to hit the muscles from every angle, so you’ll have to use at least 2 different exercises for each body part. Tuesday and Thursday are light days and can be accompanied by some form of light cardio (usually 20 mins).

Weight Loss

This is the weight loss workout. Practice proper form as you will be more effective in activating your muscles when throwing punches.YouTube has great videos demonstrating proper form for each punch. Throw as many punches as you can for each timed period. Try 3 rounds to begin.

No Weights
