Target:

  • Who: Residents, locals, and visitors of the state of Idaho; and anyone looking to find weather information about local areas in this state.
  • Age: 8 years and older.
  • Access: Mobile devices (phones, tablets, etc.), and desktop/laptops will be able to access our site.
  • Income: Income levels do not matter as everyone has a need to access weather information.
  • Motivation: Planning everyday tasks, singular, and/or recurring events in the area/state.

Persona 1

Rick Montague is an East Idaho local. He’s a family business owner: a farmer and rancher. He is 50 years old, married, father of 10 kids, and has an undergraduate degree in agriculture. As owner and operator of the family farm, he is driven by seasonal goals and timelines. He spends his work time reviewing and updating plans, calendars, and budgets; managing, directing, and supervising staff and operations; and managing logistical work. 90 percent of his time is spent in the outdoors. He carries and smart phone and tablet to connect with employees and related business owners. He is proficient in the use of mobile technology as it is a vital part of his communication system and business.

Quote: “Work smart and you will accomplish more.”

Persona 2

Timothy Timberland is a full-time student. He is 23 years old, single, the oldest of 5, and is pursuing degree in communications. He is concerned with graduating in 3 years and is taking a full load of classes. He Likes to explore Idaho’s scenery on the weekends. Tim spends his time in class or studying; hanging out with friends; and going on mini trips around East Idaho. He is comfortable using technology, but is not an expert. Most of the time he uses a laptop for news, homework, streaming media, etc.. Indoors 80 percent of the time.

Quote: “Come on, marry me!”

Scenarios

  • Rick needs to fix a fence and wants to know the upper temperature that day.
  • Rick is transporting a few horses and needs to know if it will snow.
  • Rick is checking for updates on seasonal rain, which will save him money this month.
  • Rick needs to deliver perishables; weather will determine logistical preparations and start/end times.
  • Rick is making preparations for winter and wants to know when the temperature will be below 32 degrees

Scenarios

  • Tim is walking to an early class tomorrow and wants to know how cold it will be in the morning.
  • Tim needs a new profile picture and wants to know if it will be sunny today.
  • Tim wants to go hiking this weekend, he wants to know if it will rain.
  • Tim has a date next week, the weather will decide where he will take her.
  • Tim lost his phone at the park, he wants to know how much snow to expect as he goes looking for it.
