Site Purpose

We, at Meteorological Minute, are dedicated to providing accurate and real-time weather forecasts and updates to Idaho residents and visitors. We understand the importance weather can have on planning, that is why we have developed this user-friendly site. Our purpose is to provide the best forecasting for your area in an easy to use way. Whether at the office, home, or on a trip, you can take our services wherever you go. So, if you’re a local, follow us! Or, if you’re visiting the Gem State, visit us too!
