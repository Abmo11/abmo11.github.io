Color Scheme

The primary and secondary colors will provide contrast throughout the site. The first accent color falls between the primary and secondary colors and will be a way to 'tie' these two main colors. The second accent color will make the site pop with an eye catching color. This purple was chosen to represent a shade of the sky, as the site is a meteorological site.
