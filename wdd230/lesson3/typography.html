Font

The two fonts I have chosen for this site are: Raleway, and Montserrat . Raleway will only be used in the headings. Montserrat will be used in the rest of the page. I want to create contrast throughout the site and I believe these two fonts will do that. They are clean and modern fonts. This will also add to the modern 'feel' of the site I want. This entire site was made using these fonts as outlined here.
